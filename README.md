## 📖 About

A fully interactive real-time dashboard tracking the education program reach of **SENF** across Afghanistan — from broadcast TV and radio to mobile apps and AI chatbots.

Built with pure web technologies and Google Sheets as a live backend — no server, no framework, no database. Just clean code syncing live data with a single click.

## 📈 What It Tracks

<details>
<summary><b>📡 Broadcast Reach</b></summary>

- **TV Viewers** — Annual & monthly figures
- **Radio Listeners** — Annual & monthly figures  
- **Sciencestan TV** — Annual reach & online views
- **Girls WhatsApp Tutoring** — Quarterly figures

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>📱 Digital Apps</b></summary>

- **Dana App** — Daily sign-ups, active users, course completions
- **SENF App** — Cumulative downloads, total & active users
- **Kankor App** — Total users & video minutes watched
- **Lalah Chatbot** — Total users, monthly actives & messages

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>📊 Analytics & Charts</b></summary>

- Channel distribution doughnut charts
- Viewing frequency breakdown
- Platform performance progress bars
- Combined reach comparison bar chart
- Monthly growth line charts per app

</details>
